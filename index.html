More About Dr Meenal Patel

Dr Meenal Patel BDS Hons (LON) BSc Hons (KCL)

Meenal is a friendly and highly skilled dentist who's bubbly nature and clinical expertise are known for giving patients an alternative and enjoyable experience at the dentist.

Meenal is a private general dentist with a passion for smile makeovers and advanced restorative dentistry. She has continued to work across London, Surrey and Kent where she takes on referrals for complex restorative cases and has transformed hundreds of smiles. She qualified with Honours from Bart's and The London School of Dentistry in 2012. Meenal has always continued to strive towards self-improvement and has undertaken a long list of international post-graduate training courses in Advanced Restorative and Cosmetic Dentistry.

As well as learning, Meenal enjoys teaching other dentists as a national lecturer for SmileFast. She is proud to have been awarded the prestigious "Centre of Excellence" status, being one of only 20 centres in the UK to have achieved this accolade. Amongst her friends within the dental industry, she is known as the SmileFast Queen and, more recently, the Queen of Styloso as she continues to be one of the highest providers of SmileFast makeovers in the UK and Europe.
